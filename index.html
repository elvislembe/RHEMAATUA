Welcome

RHEMAtua CBO works in Uganda’s Kyaka II Refugee Settlement and host communities to support vulnerable children, youth, women, and families through education, health, livelihoods, psychosocial support, and spiritual development.

Our Mission

To improve the well-being of vulnerable children, youth, women, and families through inclusive education, health and WASH services, sustainable livelihoods, counseling, capacity building, and spiritual guidance.

Why Support Us?

  • Transparent and accountable community programs
  • Strong grassroots presence in refugee settlements
  • Direct impact on children, women, and families
  • Partnership-ready for NGOs and international donors
